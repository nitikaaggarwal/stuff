    What is it?

    truBrain's goal is to give you the competitive edge of focused cognitive performance as an everyday alternative to excess caffeine and energy drinks. truBrain was designed by our team of Neuroscientists Dr. Andrew Hill from UCLA and Dr. Christoph Michael from the University of Geneva. See what's in it

  •  

    How does it work

    trueBrain's blend of cognitive nutrition was designed to address the fundamental components of brain chemistry. truBrain is meant to improve memory, focus and complex reasoning by increasing oxygen flow to the brain and boosting neural plasticity. We put the best of brain fitness in a convenient monthly box.

  •  

    The competitive edge of focused cognitive performance

    Each of the ingredients of our blend has a long study history of over 40 years and have been thoroughly vetted by our published neuroscientists. Each component has a long record without material side-effects. We also take care to exceed the highest standards of quality assurance set by the Food & Drug Administration.
